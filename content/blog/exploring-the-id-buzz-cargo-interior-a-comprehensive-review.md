## Modern Connectivity and Digital Systems

The Volkswagen ID Buzz Cargo is equipped with an array of modern connectivity and digital systems designed to enhance the functionality and ease of use for its users. These features are not only innovative but are tailored to meet the demands of a modern commercial vehicle.

### Digital Cockpit

The ID Buzz Cargo boasts a Digital Cockpit, a feature that streamlines the driving experience by placing crucial vehicle information directly in front of the driver. This system integrates digital dials that display a variety of data such as speed and range, ensuring that all necessary information is easily accessible. This setup mirrors the intuitive designs found in other Volkswagen products, where a small screen positioned directly ahead complements a larger touchscreen to the left. This arrangement allows drivers to stay informed with minimal distraction from the road.

### Infotainment Features

Central to the ID Buzz Cargo’s appeal is its sophisticated infotainment system, featured prominently on the centre console. This system includes a large screen that offers crisp graphics, enhancing user interaction despite the smaller icons and fonts which can be challenging to operate while driving. The infotainment system is occasionally sluggish in response, which is a minor drawback in an otherwise advanced setup. Standard features such as Apple CarPlay and Android Auto enhance connectivity, allowing seamless integration of smartphone functionalities. Higher trims like the Commerce Plus add ‘Discover Pro’ navigation, providing detailed route guidance essential for commercial usage. Additional amenities in these trims include adaptive cruise control and a rear-view camera, augmenting both convenience and safety.

The integration of these digital systems in the ID Buzz Cargo underscores Volkswagen’s commitment to combining traditional vehicle utility with advanced technology, making it an ideal choice for professionals seeking a reliable and forward-thinking transport solution. The vehicle supports a variety of digital features that are crucial for day-to-day operations, ensuring that it remains a top contender in the realm of commercial electric vehicles.

## Cargo Space and Functionality

### Load Compartment

The ID Buzz Cargo excels in maximising space utilisation within its compact dimensions. With a cargo hold volume of 3.9 cubic metres, it matches the demands of various commercial activities. The load compartment floor stretches to 2,208 mm when accessed via the tailgate and slightly extends to 2,232 mm with hinged doors, accommodating a range of cargo sizes. The maximum loading width stands at 1,732 mm, reducing to 1,230 mm between the wheel housings, which is crucial for fitting standard-sized equipment or materials. The compartment’s height reaches 1,257 mm, ensuring ample vertical space for stacking and storage.

### Transport Features

The ID Buzz Cargo is designed with practicality in mind, featuring a low loading sill height of 623 mm, which facilitates easier loading and unloading of heavy or bulky items. This model can transport a maximum payload ranging from 516 kg to 647 kg, depending on configuration, which, while not the highest in its class, supports efficient urban commercial transport. Its ability to accommodate two Euro pallets underscores its utility in daily operations. Additionally, the vehicle includes a basic wooden lining and floor in the loading bay, providing immediate use without the need for initial modifications. The inclusion of a folding towing bracket enhances its versatility, allowing for the towing of additional loads up to 1,000 kg, complemented by a minimal turning circle of 11.1 metres that aids in navigating tight spaces.

By simply addressing the system with “Hello ID.”, drivers can control the radio, navigation, air conditioning, and other vehicle functions effortlessly. This system is distinguished by its ability to understand colloquial expressions and free formulations, moving beyond fixed voice commands. Complementing the voice control, the ID. Buzz Cargo features a multifunction steering wheel wrapped in high-quality leatherette, which provides not only comfort but also practicality. Drivers can also control the DAB+ radio, navigation system, and smartphone connectivity, as well as activate the speed limiter directly from the steering wheel. For added convenience during colder months, the optional steering wheel heating ensures comfort, maintaining a pleasant temperature for the hands.

The integration of these technologies in the ID. Buzz Cargo not only enhances the functionality of the vehicle but also ensures a more connected and intuitive driving experience. ## Sustainability and Electric Drive

The Volkswagen ID Buzz Cargo sets a benchmark in electric vehicle innovation with its sustainable design and efficient electric drive. This section explores the sustainability features and the electric drive that power the ID Buzz Cargo, emphasizing its role in promoting environmentally friendly commercial transportation.

### Electric Motor

The ID Buzz Cargo is equipped with a modern electric motor positioned in the rear, which not only maximizes space but also enhances the vehicle’s dynamics. With a maximum output of 150 kW, equivalent to 204 PS, the motor ensures the van is dynamic and robust, meeting the daily demands of both private and commercial users. This powerful setup allows the ID Buzz Cargo to reach a maximum speed of 145 km/h and tow up to 1,000 kg with the optional towing equipment. The motor’s instant torque of 310 Nm makes the vehicle exceptionally responsive and capable of handling various transportation tasks efficiently.

### Charging Capabilities

Volkswagen has integrated advanced charging technology into the ID Buzz Cargo to ensure it supports the fast-paced needs of commercial use. The van features a lithium-ion high-voltage battery located within the vehicle floor, boasting a net energy content of 77 kWh (gross: 82 kWh). This substantial capacity enables the vehicle to travel up to 425 kilometres on a single charge, making it ideal for long-distance hauls without frequent stops for charging.

For regular charging, the ID Buzz Cargo accommodates up to 11 kW AC charging, which is suitable for overnight charging or top-ups during work hours. However, its DC fast charging capability is particularly noteworthy, supporting up to 170 kW. This allows the battery to be charged from 5% to 80% in approximately 30 minutes, ideal for quick recharges during short breaks. The implementation of Plug & Charge technology simplifies the charging process further by eliminating the need for authentication at compatible charging stations; the vehicle automatically starts charging once the cable is connected.

Moreover, the cost-effectiveness of charging the ID Buzz Cargo is enhanced when paired with an EV-friendly energy tariff. With the right tariff and a smart charger, users can significantly reduce the cost of charging, making it not only an eco-friendly choice but also an economically wise one for businesses.

These features of the ID Buzz Cargo’s electric drive and charging capabilities highlight Volkswagen’s commitment to sustainability and practicality in their commercial vehicle lineup, ensuring that businesses can rely on the ID Buzz Cargo for their transportation needs while also contributing to environmental conservation.
