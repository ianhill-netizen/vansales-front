## Choosing The Best Van for Road Trips – What You Need to Consider

Choosing the most ideal van for your off-road adventure in the UK shouldn’t be difficult, bearing in mind what your preferences are. Let’s have a look at the factors that you need to look into it in detail:

### a)   The van capacity

The choice of vans for road trips as far as capacity is concerned, is dependent on how many companions or how much luggage you’ll bring to your journey. For larger groups of up to eight people, you might want to consider something with a minimum of-passenger capacity. And an excellent example here is Volkswagen Transporter Kombi.

On the other hand, if it’s a solo or two-person road trip, you may opt for a small van with a compact interior and more manoeuvrability.

### b)   Comfort and amenities

Planning to spend longer hours driving? The experience can be nerve-wracking. But with a well-equipped and comfortable van, your road trip will be more enjoyable than tiring. Check out for features such as comfy ergonomic seats, entertainment options, and climate control.

![Comfort and amenities ](/images/blog/Comfort-and-amenities.jpg)

Also, if you’ll be spending your nights in the van, you might want to consider conversion vans for road trips as they offer more sleeping comfort and luxury.

### c)    Manoeuvrability and handling

The best van for road trips should have easier navigation and manoeuvrability on narrow streets and tight parking spaces. During your UK road trip, you’ll experience different situations with demanding handling challenges. If your van of choice offers better handling, you’ll be more confident on the road and have an easier time navigating any street.

### d)   Fuel efficiency

When planning long road trips that could go beyond borders, the fuel efficiency of your chosen van needs to be at the top of your list. With more fuel-efficient vans, you can significantly reduce your overall road trip cost with lower environmental impact.

With electric vans booming in the market, you can as well opt for a cost-friendly hybrid option to save your overall fuel expense. The Ford Transit Custom Plug-in Hybrid (PHEV) makes an excellent option, featuring a.0-litre EcoBoost petrol engine with a.6kWh Li-ion battery-powered motor. Alternatively, the fully electric IVECO Daily Electric can cover up tokm on a full charge and can equally serve as a camper van for road trips.

These are among the key considerations among other factors you may want to look into when looking for an off-road van. Other factors will mostly depend on your preferences, such as the availability of storage compartments, et cetera.
