## FAQs

**1\. Can you fully deduct van leasing from your taxes?**  
Yes, leasing a van for business purposes allows you to deduct 100% of the lease payments as a business expense. This is because you are renting the van rather than owning it, making it a deductible ongoing expense, provided you meet all the necessary criteria.

**2\. How much does it cost to lease a van?**  
You can find competitive van leasing deals typically ranging from £150 to £250 per month. This makes it a cost-effective way to drive a van that suits your needs without the high upfront cost of purchasing.

**3\. Is leasing a van a sensible choice?**  
Leasing a van can be a wise financial decision, particularly for businesses. It usually offers lower monthly payments compared to purchasing a van outright, which can help maintain better cash flow. Essentially, you pay for the van’s use in manageable instalments over time.

**4\. What is the process for leasing a van for business use?**  
To lease a van for your business, the process is similar to personal leasing. You select the van or fleet you need and agree to make regular monthly payments for the duration of the lease agreement. The leasing company will assess your financial stability to ensure you can sustain the payments throughout the term.
