## Exterior Dimensions

The Mercedes-Benz Sprinter Van is meticulously crafted to accommodate a variety of needs, offering flexibility in exterior dimensions to suit both commercial and personal applications. ### Length Options

The Sprinter Van comes in multiple length configurations, which are primarily determined by the wheelbase. The available lengths are:

1.  **Compact (L1)**: This model, with a typical wheelbase of 3665 millimeters, is the most compact version, providing limited interior space and is less common for campervan conversions.
2.  **Standard (L2)**: With a wheelbase of 3529 millimeters, this variant strikes a balance between size and maneuverability, making it popular for diverse applications including campervan builds.
3.  **Long (L3)**: The L3 extends approximately 1 meter longer than the L2, offering enhanced interior capacity suitable for families or more elaborate campervan setups.
4.  **Extra Long (L4)**: This option adds an additional 40 centimeters to the interior length compared to the L3, catering to those needing maximum space for group travel or specialized conversions.

The specific exterior lengths in inches for these models range from 233.5 inches on the shorter end to 290 inches for the extended versions.

### Height Options

The Sprinter Van’s height varies depending on the roof configuration chosen, with three primary options available:

1.  **Standard Roof (H1)**: This roof type is ideal for environments with height restrictions, offering a more compact profile that facilitates access to tighter spaces.
2.  **High Roof (H2)**: Most popular for conversions, the high roof provides ample headroom, allowing most individuals to stand comfortably inside the van.
3.  **Super High Roof (H3)**: Offering the most vertical space, this option is perfect for taller individuals or those requiring maximum storage capacity.

Height measurements range from 2.35 meters for the standard roof to 2.83 meters for the super high roof, providing flexibility in vehicle height based on usage needs.

### Width Options

The width of the Sprinter Van remains consistent across various models, ensuring stability and robustness. The total width, including mirrors, measures 95.5 inches. However, the body width without mirrors stands at 80 inches. For scenarios requiring narrower configurations, the width with folded mirrors is 85.6 inches, which can be crucial for navigating through tight spaces.

These dimensions collectively make the Mercedes-Benz Sprinter a versatile vehicle, adaptable to a wide range of applications from cargo transport to luxury mobile homes, ensuring that each model can be tailored to meet specific dimensional needs without compromising on performance or comfort.

## Comparing Different Models

### Short Wheelbase

The Short-Wheelbase (SWB) Sprinter, known as the L1H1, measures 5267mm in overall length, 2020mm in width, and 2356mm in height. It provides a load volume of 7.8m³, making it suitable for moderate hauling tasks. The internal maximum load length stands at 2732mm, catering to those who require a compact yet functional vehicle for city navigation and smaller cargo loads.

### Medium Wheelbase

The Medium-Wheelbase (MWB) Sprinter offers more versatility, measuring 5932mm in length. It comes in two roof heights: the H1 at 2351mm and the H2 at 2638mm for FWD and 2630mm for RWD models. All MWB models maintain a width of 2020mm. The load volumes vary with configuration: 9.5m³ for L2H1 and 11m³ for L2H2 FWD, slightly reduced to 10.5m³ for RWD. The maximum internal load length is 3397mm for FWD and 3272mm for RWD, providing a balance between maneuverability and cargo capacity.

### Long Wheelbase

The Long-Wheelbase (LWB) Sprinter, or L3, extends to a length of 6967mm. This model skips the H1 roof option, offering H2 and H3 heights at 2616mm and 2831mm respectively. The consistent width of 2020mm supports the vehicle’s robust framework. The L3H2 and L3H3 models boast load volumes of 14m³ and 15.5m³, respectively, with a maximum load length of 4410mm, making it ideal for larger transport needs and extensive modifications.

### Extra-Long Wheelbase

The Extra-Long-Wheelbase (XLWB) Sprinter, or L4, reaches a length of 7367mm. It also does not feature an H1 roof, with the H2 standing at 2613mm and the H3 at 2825mm in height. The width remains at 2020mm across all models. The L4H2 and L4H3 provide substantial load volumes of 15.5m³ and 17m³, respectively, with the maximum load length peaking at 4810mm. This variant is tailored for those requiring the utmost in cargo space and is particularly popular for extensive camper conversions or specialized transport services.

Each model of the Mercedes Sprinter brings distinct advantages, catering to a wide range of commercial and personal applications, from compact urban transport solutions in the SWB to expansive cargo handling in the XLWB. ## FAQs Mercedes Sprinter Dimensions

**What are the specific dimensions of a Mercedes Sprinter van?**  
The Mercedes Sprinter van has a consistent width of 95.5 inches and a height of 94.4 inches. It offers two main length options based on the wheelbase: the 144-inch wheelbase model is 245.9 inches long, while the 170-inch wheelbase model is 273.5 inches long.

**What is the length of a full-size Mercedes Sprinter van?**  
In the United States, Mercedes Sprinter vans are available in two lengths. The shorter version, with a 144-inch wheelbase, measures 233.5 inches in length. The longer version, with a 170-inch wheelbase, measures 274.3 inches in length.

**Are Mercedes Sprinter vans available in different sizes?**  
Yes, Mercedes Sprinter vans are available in four main wheelbase sizes: Compact, Standard, Long, and Extra Long. These sizes affect the overall vehicle length and the interior space available.

**What are the dimensions of the Mercedes Sprinter van with a 170-inch wheelbase?**  
The Sprinter van with a 170-inch wheelbase has an overall height of 107.3 inches, a length of 274.3 inches, and a width including mirrors of 92.3 inches.
