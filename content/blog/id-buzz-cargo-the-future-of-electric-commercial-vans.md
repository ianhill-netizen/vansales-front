## Technology and Connectivity

### Infotainment Systems

The ID Buzz Cargo is equipped with an advanced infotainment system designed for ease of use and efficient management of both the vehicle and connectivity features. Users can navigate through a straightforward main menu to access various functions. For instance, pairing a smartphone is seamless; one simply goes to settings, selects ‘mobile devices’, and chooses the device to pair. Multiple mobile phones can be connected, with the option to switch between them effortlessly.

The integration of app connectivity is another highlight. When stationary, users can connect their mobile via ‘app connect’, allowing them to download additional applications such as Spotify. However, this requires an active and linked We Connect account. The infotainment system also offers personalisation options such as ambient lighting adjustments, accessible via the ‘background’ icon in the main menu, where users can select from various colours and light themes to enhance the cabin atmosphere.

Moreover, the system provides easy access to essential features like phone connectivity, radio, navigation, vehicle settings, sound settings, exterior light settings, and charging information, ensuring that everything a driver needs is just a touch away. Below the infotainment screen, shortcut buttons provide quick access to climate control, driving modes, and driver assistance systems, further enhancing the user experience.

### Driver Assistance Features

The ID Buzz Cargo introduces a suite of sophisticated driver assistance features, enhancing both safety and convenience. The vehicle supports over-the-air updates, ensuring that systems such as infotainment, charging, and driver assist are always up-to-date without the need to visit a service centre.

One of the standout features is the Travel Assist, which includes adaptive cruise control and lane keep assist. This system not only maintains the vehicle in its lane and controls the distance from the vehicle ahead but also adapts to the driving style and road conditions. It can adjust the vehicle’s speed according to the road’s layout and recognised speed limits. Additionally, if swarm data is available, Travel Assist can operate with just one detected road lane marking, increasing its utility on country roads without central lane markings.

Park Assist Plus with Memory Function is another innovative feature. It allows the vehicle to learn and repeat up to five individual parking manoeuvres. Once a parking procedure is saved, the ID Buzz Cargo can perform the parking manoeuvre autonomously, requiring the driver only to monitor the process. This system is particularly useful for regular parking activities, such as in a garage or a carport.

Furthermore, the vehicle includes a comprehensive array of safety systems like Car2X, which communicates with other vehicles and infrastructure to warn of hazards, Front Assist with pedestrian and cyclist monitoring, and PreCrash occupant protection, which prepares the vehicle and its occupants in the event of an imminent collision.

These advanced technology and connectivity features in the ID Buzz Cargo not only enhance the driving experience but also promise increased safety and efficiency, making it a top contender in the electric commercial vehicle sector.
