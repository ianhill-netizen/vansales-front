The VAT implications of owning a Land Rover Defender Commercial are significant for businesses. For the Land Rover Defender Commercial, the ability to reclaim VAT depends on several factors:.  Classification: If the vehicle is classified as a van or commercial vehicle for VAT purposes, businesses may be able to reclaim the full VAT..  Intended use: HMRC considers the intended use of commercial vehicles that are suitable for private use.
