## Technical Specifications

The Mercedes Vito 2024 boasts a range of technical enhancements that cater to both power and efficiency, setting a new standard in the van market. This section will explore the engine options, highlight the electric and diesel variations, and detail the transmission systems available.

### Engine Options

The Vito 2024 is equipped with robust 2.0-litre four-cylinder diesel engines, designed to handle heavy loads with ease. The base model, the 114 CDI, delivers a commendable 136 horsepower and 243 lb-ft of torque. For those seeking more power, the top-tier 119 CDI offers an impressive 190 horsepower and 324 lb-ft of torque. These engines are engineered for responsiveness and minimal effort under load, ensuring a smooth and powerful driving experience.

### Electric and Diesel Variations

Aligning with modern demands for sustainability and performance, the Vito 2024 is available in both diesel and electric variants. The all-electric eVito stands out with its zero-emissions powertrain, providing instant torque for brisk acceleration and maintaining exceptional quietness at lower speeds. This model boasts an electric range of 162 miles on a single charge, making it an ideal choice for businesses looking to minimize their environmental impact without sacrificing operational capability.

### Transmission Details

The Vito 2024 features the advanced 9G-TRONIC 9-speed automatic transmission on its higher trims. This transmission system, originally developed for Mercedes-Benz passenger cars, is renowned for its smooth and almost imperceptible shifting. It enhances the driving experience by seamlessly adapting to different driving conditions, ensuring optimal performance and fuel efficiency.

These technical specifications of the Mercedes Vito 2024 underline its evolution as a versatile vehicle that does not compromise on power, efficiency, or environmental responsibility. ## Safety and Driver Assistance

### Standard Safety Systems

The Mercedes Vito 2024 comes standard with a comprehensive suite of safety technologies that have notably excelled in the Euro NCAP’s rigorous testing protocols. The vehicle has achieved a Platinum safety rating, a significant improvement from its previous Silver rating. This enhancement is partly due to the inclusion of the Autonomous Emergency Braking (AEB) system across all European models. This system is adept at detecting vehicles, pedestrians, and cyclists, providing timely alerts and braking interventions to prevent collisions. Performance remains robust even at higher speeds or when targets are partially obscured.

The Vito also features a standard-fit Lane Support System (LSS) that actively assists in keeping the vehicle within its lane. This system gently steers the van back if it begins to drift and can perform more aggressive maneuvers in critical situations to prevent roadway departures. Additionally, a speed assistance system utilizes digital mapping and onboard cameras to identify local speed limits, allowing drivers to set speed limiters accordingly, enhancing compliance and safety.

### Advanced Driver Assist Features

Mercedes-Benz has equipped the Vito with advanced driver assistance systems that underscore its commitment to safety and technological innovation. The Vito includes features such as Active Brake Assist, Blind Spot Assist, and Lane Keeping Assist as standard. These systems work synergistically to provide a safer driving environment by monitoring blind spots, maintaining lane integrity, and supporting braking in critical situations.

Furthermore, the vehicle offers an optional driving assistance package that elevates safety to new heights. This package includes adaptive cruise control, a 360-degree camera system, and active speed limit assist, which together provide a more relaxed and controlled driving experience. For parking and maneuvering in tight spaces, the Vito can be outfitted with park assist, pedestrian alert, and a trailer assist system, all designed to reduce the risk of accidents and enhance driver confidence.

### Special Safety Enhancements

In addition to its robust standard safety features, the Mercedes Vito 2024 includes several enhancements that further solidify its position as a leader in vehicle safety. The integration of a reversing camera ensures better visibility when backing up, crucial for a commercial van’s functionality. The vehicle’s construction includes a pre-safe system and impulse side, which prepare the vehicle and its occupants for a collision by adjusting the seats, tightening seat belts, and closing windows to provide optimal protection during an impact.

Moreover, the advanced emergency stop assist feature is particularly noteworthy. This system can bring the vehicle to a complete stop if it detects that the driver is unresponsive, using lane markings and traffic conditions to safely halt the Vito without causing disruption on the road. This feature not only enhances safety for the driver and passengers but also for other road users.

These enhancements, along with the Vito’s robust build quality, may contribute to lower insurance premiums, providing economic benefits alongside increased safety.
